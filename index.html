About me

My name is Hunter Leary. I'm a graduate student in computer science. My research is in the software engineering domain. I grew up under the Blue Ridge mountains and have pretty much stayed around them my whole life. I got my BS in Software Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Virginia's College at Wise. Currently, I'm at Virginia Tech working with Dr. Chris Brown.
